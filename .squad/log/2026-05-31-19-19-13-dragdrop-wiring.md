## Issue

Drag-and-drop failed when dragging files over the WPF UI drop zone. Previous UIPI fix (ba4c336) added AllowDrop to DropZoneBorder but not the handlers, making the border the OLE target with no confirmation logic.

## Solution

Wired DragEnter, DragOver, DragLeave, Drop handlers directly to DropZoneBorder in MainWindow.xaml. Reused existing code-behind methods.

- 0 code-behind changes
